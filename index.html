What does this do?

It sends a micro-transaction (0.001) to a dead treasury address with a permanent message attached ("FLAGGED"). This marks the wallet activity on blockchain explorers forever.

Is it anonymous?

Yes. You don't need to create an account or give us your email. You just sign a transaction with the compromised wallet to prove you own it (or owned it).

Why do it?

To prove to exchanges, protocols, and friends that this address is no longer under your control. It's the ultimate "DO NOT INTERACT" sign.
